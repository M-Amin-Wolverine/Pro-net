## â“ Frequently Asked Questions
<details>
<summary><b>Q: How does MeshVPN differ from traditional VPNs?</b></summary>
A: Traditional VPNs rely on central servers (single point of failure). MeshVPN creates a peer-to-peer mesh where each device can route traffic, providing true decentralization, lower latency for local peers, and continued operation even if parts of the network go down.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Q: Is my data safe with random peers?</b></summary>
A: Yes! All traffic is end-to-end encrypted with WireGuard-grade cryptography. Peers only see encrypted packets they cannot decrypt. Additionally, multi-hop routing ensures no single peer sees both source and destination.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Q: What about battery life?</b></summary>
A: MeshVPN uses adaptive discovery intervals and low-power Bluetooth LE for nearby peer discovery. In typical usage, battery impact is 2-4% per hour, comparable to streaming music.
</details>
